Nanofabrication Lab

In the nanofabrication lab, we completed four projects increasing in difficulty: Schottky Diode, LED, Photodetector & MOSFET


Here, I will display our culminating project, the creation of a metal-oxide field-effect transistors, or MOSFET. A MOSFET is a special transistor that use an electrostatic effect from a gate rather than simply a stackup of PN junctions to control switching.


Some of the processes we needed to do to complete the MOSFET included:

  • Photolithography
  • Buffered Oxide Etch (BOE)
  • Plasma Enhanced Chemical Vapor Deposition (PECVD)
  • Reactive-ion Etching (RIE)
  • Atomic Layer Deposition (ALD)
  • Sputtering
  • Profilometry
  • Microscopy
  • Electronic Probing
