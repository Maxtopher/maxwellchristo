Segmented Bat

Segmented Bat

Materials: Ash, Black Walnut, Cherry & Purple Heart

This is a baseball bat constructed an ash handle and 39 rings, each containing 12 segments. By organizing the different segments made from distinctly different colors and intentionally layering the rings, a unique pattern is formed on the bat's barrel.
