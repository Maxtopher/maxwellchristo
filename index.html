Maxwell Christopher

Hello! I'm Maxwell Christopher, a recent graduate from Harvard University, where I concentrated in physics and mathematics, with a secondary in astrophysics. I'm fascinated by the natural world's order and patterns, which drive my passion to unite art and science in my future studies.

Beyond academics, I'm an unconventional artist, seeing potential for creativity and beauty in unexpected places. I'm particularly drawn to woodworking, where I craft meaningful pieces in a blend of creativity and precision. Alongside my creative pursuits, I find solace and challenge in long-distance running and cycling, balancing physical strains with the mental fortitude of endurance sports.

Looking ahead, I aim to continue exploring the intersection of art and science, seeking innovative ways to merge these fields and contribute to our understanding of the natural world.
